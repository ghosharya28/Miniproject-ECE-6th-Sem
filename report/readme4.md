## Purpose

The primary objective of this documentation is to present the design, implementation, and experimental validation of a low-cost DDS-based RF instrumentation platform using readily available hardware. The project demonstrates RF signal generation, wire antenna characterization, and passive RLC component analysis while establishing a foundation for future communication and RF measurement systems. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
